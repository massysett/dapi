dapi - print a series of dates

This is a little program that prints a series of dates to standard output. The name is short for "date picker". You also get a sophisticated filtering mechanism to remove dates you do not want to see.

Examples:

  • dapi 'this month'
  • dapi 'next year'
  • dapi '+2 week'

To show each day next month that is a Tuesday:

dapi --weekday '==' tue 'next month'

Plenty of examples are in the manpage.
